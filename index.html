Our Courses

We offer the following courses.

FOUNDATION

A foundation course, also known as a foundation course or pre-university courses, is an academic course designed to prepare students for entry into an undergraduate degree course. It is typically aimed at students who have completed their secondary education but may not meet the direct entry requirements for their desired bachelor's degree course.

UNDERGRADUATE

An undergraduate course refers to a degree course that students pursue after completing their secondary education. Undergraduate courses typically offer bachelor's degrees, which are awarded upon completion of the course.
Our course offers -
1 - BA (HONS) Business & Management
2 - BSc (HONS) Computer Science
3 - BSc (HONS) Psychology
4 - B.S.N (HONS) Nursing
5 - B.TECH (HONS) Technology

POSTGRADUATE

A postgraduate course, also known as a graduate course, is an educational course that follows the completion of an undergraduate degree. It is designed to provide advanced knowledge, specialized skills, and research opportunities in a specific field of study. Postgraduate course are typically pursued at the master's level.
Our course offers -
1 - MA (HONS) Business & Management
2 - MSc (HONS) Computer Science
3 - MSc (HONS) Psychology
4 - Msc (HONS) Nursing
5 - M.TECH (HONS) Technology
